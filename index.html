Good to know

Questions, answered.

Do I need to be flexible already? +

Not at all — the opposite. The tighter you are, the more you'll feel and see. We meet your body where it is and build from there.

What do I wear? +

Comfortable athletic clothing you can move in — the same thing you'd wear to the gym. No special gear needed.

What actually happens in a session? +

You lie on a padded table while Deveon guides your body through targeted stretches you can't reach on your own, using leverage and hands-on cues. It's active, relaxing, and completely one-on-one.

How often should I come in? +

Most members see the best results with 1–2 sessions per week, paired with a simple at-home mobility routine we build for you.

Is this like a massage? +

Related, but different. Massage works your tissue while you're passive; assisted stretching actively moves your joints through range to build lasting mobility — not just temporary relief.
