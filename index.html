Good to know

Questions, answered.

Do you really come to me? +

Yes — Mr. Stretch is fully mobile. Deveon brings the table and everything needed to your home, gym, office, or event anywhere across the Houston metro area. Prefer a studio setting? That can be arranged too.

Do I need to be flexible already? +

Not at all — the opposite. The tighter you are, the more you'll feel and see. We meet your body where it is and build from there.

What do I wear, and what do I need? +

Comfortable athletic clothing you can move in — the same thing you'd wear to the gym. Just a bit of open floor space; we bring the table and everything else.

Can you do my team or office? +

Absolutely. Mr. Stretch does on-site corporate and team sessions — offices, sports teams, and events across the Houston metro. Reach out with your group size and we'll build a plan.

How often should I book? +

Most clients see the best results with 1–2 sessions per week, paired with a simple at-home mobility routine we build for you.

Is this like a massage? +

Related, but different. Massage works your tissue while you're passive; assisted stretching actively moves your joints through range to build lasting mobility — not just temporary relief.
